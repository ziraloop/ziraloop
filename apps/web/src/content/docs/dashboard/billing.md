# Billing & Plans

Manage your LLMVault subscription, view usage, and access invoices.

## Current Plan

Navigate to **Settings > Billing** to view plan details.

### Plan Card

The current plan section displays:
- Plan name (Free, Pro, or Enterprise)
- Active status badge
- Renewal date

## Available Plans

### Free Plan

**Price:** $0/month

**Includes:**
- 15 credentials
- 10,000 proxy requests/month
- 500 identities
- 7-day audit log
- 3 team members

**Best for:** Evaluation, small projects, personal use

### Pro Plan

**Price:** $49/month

**Includes:**
- 100 credentials
- 100,000 proxy requests/month
- 5,000 identities
- 90-day audit log
- Priority support
- 10 team members

**Best for:** Growing teams, production workloads

### Enterprise Plan

**Price:** Custom

**Includes:**
- Unlimited credentials
- Unlimited proxy requests
- Unlimited identities
- 1-year audit log
- SSO & SAML
- Dedicated support
- Custom contracts

**Best for:** Large organizations, compliance requirements

## Upgrading

### To Pro

1. Click **"Upgrade"** on the Pro plan card
2. Enter payment information
3. Confirm upgrade
4. Features immediately available

### To Enterprise

1. Click **"Contact Sales"**
2. Fill out contact form
3. Sales team will reach out within 24 hours
4. Custom pricing and contract negotiation

## Usage Tracking

Monitor usage against plan limits:

**Free Plan Sidebar Widget:**
- Credentials: X / 15
- Proxy Requests: X / 10,000
- Identities: X / 500

Progress bars show usage percentage with color coding.

**Reset Schedule:**
- Usage resets monthly
- Reset date displayed (e.g., "Resets in 18 days")

## Overage Handling

| Plan | Overage Behavior |
|------|------------------|
| Free | Requests blocked when limit reached |
| Pro | Requests blocked; upgrade option presented |
| Enterprise | No limits; custom overage terms |

## Invoices

The invoices section displays billing history:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| ID | Invoice identifier (e.g., `inv_001`) |
| Date | Invoice date |
| Amount | Total amount |
| Status | Paid, Pending, Failed |

### Invoice Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Paid | Payment completed successfully |
| Pending | Payment is being processed |
| Failed | Payment was unsuccessful |

### Downloading Invoices

Click an invoice row to:
- View invoice details
- Download PDF
- Print

## Payment Methods

Manage payment methods in the billing settings:
- Add credit card
- Set default payment method
- Remove expired cards
- View billing address

## Cancellation

To cancel your subscription:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until period end

**Post-Cancellation:**
- Account downgrades to Free plan
- Excess resources may be restricted
- Data is preserved
- Can resubscribe anytime

## Billing FAQ

**Q: When am I billed?**
A: Monthly on your subscription anniversary date.

**Q: Can I change plans mid-cycle?**
A: Yes, prorated charges or credits applied.

**Q: Do unused requests roll over?**
A: No, quotas reset monthly.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: The Free plan is always available. Pro trial available on request.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), ACH (Enterprise), wire transfer (Enterprise).
